OUTDOOR TIPS

Whether you're planning a weekend hike or a multi-day expedition, these practical insights will ensure you're ready for anything the outdoors throws your way.

Plan Your Adventure

Research your destination thoroughly—know the trail conditions, weather forecast, and any permits required. A well-planned trip is a safe trip!

Pack Smart, Pack Light

Carry only the essentials: multi-functional gear, a first aid kit, and lightweight clothing layers. A well-packed bag reduces strain and keeps you mobile.

Stay Hydrated and Fueled

Bring enough water or a reliable filtration system, and pack calorie-dense snacks like nuts, jerky, or energy bars to keep your energy levels up.

Master Basic Navigation

Never rely solely on your smartphone—carry a map and compass, and know how to use them. GPS devices can fail, but traditional tools are reliable.
